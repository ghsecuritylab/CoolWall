- Download the repository
    wget -t -0 -c ftp://ftp.altera.com/outgoing/uClinux-dist-20080131.tar
    
   Original: git://sopc.et.ntust.edu.tw/git/uClinux-dist.git
   AlteraWiki: http://www.alterawiki.com/wiki/Git_Server

- Extract & checkout
    . tar xvf uClinux-dist-20080131.tar
    . cd uClinux-dist || git checkout nios2

- Copy configuration files & vendor PTF file
    . ecu2.ptf
    . config/.config
    . linux-2.6.x/.config

- Config & build
    . Preinstalled in system: lynx text-based web browser
    . make config
	> Vendor/Product Selection -->
            . Vendor (Altera)
            . Altera Products (niosnommu)
        > Kernel is linux-2.6.x
            . Libc Version (None)
        > [*]Customize Kernel Settings
        > [*]Customize Application/Library Settings

    . make verdor_hwselect SYSPTF=/home/xxx/uClinux-dist/ecu2.ptf
